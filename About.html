About Us – Organic Essence

Organic Essence is a proudly SECP-registered company committed to creating 100% organic hair care products rooted in the wisdom of nature. Our mission is to offer safe, chemical-free alternatives that restore and enhance natural hair health using time-tested herbal ingredients. Each product is crafted with care, ensuring purity, effectiveness, and sustainability. We specialize in all-natural shampoo + conditioner formulas that double as hair treatments—perfect for those seeking visible, lasting results without compromising on health or quality.
Our Bestsellers:
1. Orgyano Gold – Shampoo + Conditioner + Treatment Our most popular product Orgyano Gold, is an all-in-one organic solution that works as a shampoo, conditioner and deep hair treatment. It helps reduce hair fall, fights dandruff, and nourishes the scalp—leaving your hair stronger, softer, and healthier. Key Ingredients: Amla, Reetha, Shikakai, Black Seeds, Balcher, Nager Motha, Food-grade Glycerin Flax Seeds, Aloe Vera, Almond Solution, Vitamin E.
